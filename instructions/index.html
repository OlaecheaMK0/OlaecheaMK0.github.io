Instructions

The main task of this assignment was to make proper written instructions paired with personal visuals and a short analysis of who the reader would be (our audience). I wrote a 27-step recipe for a Raspberry Chocolate Mousse Tart, broken into five phases from making the crust to assembling it all together. The audience is expected to be amateur home cooks with basic reading and kitchen skills, and it will likely be read on a phone or a tablet.

Document

Reflection

The design choices that came directly from audience analysis were making sure it was accessible and visible. For accessibility, I didn't expect every amateur home chef would have on hand all the sophisticated and expensive equipment my research came up with, so I would provide low-tech, readily available alternatives so that they wouldn't have to stop mid-project. Following this, I also made sure it would properly fit the use case of having to look at a small screen with dirty fingers. I achieved that by making sure the diagrams are visible even in bright environments, and I numbered each of the steps and substeps to make sure it would be easy to resume by the last number you were on.
